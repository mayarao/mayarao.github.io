MAYA / WRITER

Hey there! I'm Maya. Welcome to my travel page!

I've been traveling for pretty much as long as I can remember. When my brother and I were little, my parents would take us on all the trips they wanted to go on, which means that I've caught the travel bug myself. Unfortunately, and surprisingly, I have no travel-friendly skills (e.g. I still can't sleep on a plane, I've left several of my belongings on a plane), but what I am is an obsessive planner. And I mean obsessive.


One benefit of that obsessiveness, though, is that I am that friend. You know, the friend with the amazing recommendations! I made this site to share all my recommendations, experiences and itineraries for my friends and family, and you, my dear reader.


I hope this helps you plan your next amazing trip! Sending you love & good luck!
